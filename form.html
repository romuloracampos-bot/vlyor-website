The Emulsifier Argument

Everything in cosmetics
is, at its core, an
emulsification problem.

A moisturiser is oil-in-water. A sunscreen stick is water-in-oil. A serum is an aqueous gel. Every product category is defined by how its phases are held together — and the answer to that question is emulsifier selection. Choose the wrong emulsifier and the formula separates, discolours or fails stability within weeks. Choose the right one and you have a product that survives the LATAM supply chain: 3–6 months from manufacturing to consumer, through temperature extremes from Amazonian humidity to Andean cold.

VLYOR FORM addresses this problem from every angle. FormEssence fatty alcohol and castor oil ethoxylates (HLB 6–19) cover the O/W emulsifier and solubiliser spectrum. SorbEssence sorbitan esters (HLB 1.8–8.6) provide the W/O emulsifier and co-emulsifier range. FormBase ceteareth and steareth emulsifiers deliver the proven classical O/W emulsifier bases. RheoForm carbomers and biopolymers build the gel architecture. EsterSilk, TricaPure and SqualEssence complete formulas with emollient character that defines sensory profile.

"An emulsifier is not a commodity — it is a structural decision. The same oil phase, the same active and the same water phase will produce completely different stability, sensory and performance profiles depending solely on which emulsifier you choose."

FormEssence O

Fatty Alcohol Ethoxylate (C12–C18 + nEO) · Grades: O-3 · O-5 · O-8 · O-9 · O-10 · O-15 · O-20 · O-30 · O-50

Nonionic EmulsifierHLB 6–199 GradesO/W + Solubiliser

"Nine fatty alcohol ethoxylate grades covering HLB 6 to 19 — the most versatile nonionic emulsifier series in the FORM portfolio, for O/W emulsions, fragrance solubilisation and clear aqueous formulations."

FormEssence O Series is a range of fatty alcohol ethoxylates (FAE) produced by ethoxylation of C12–C18 linear fatty alcohols with varying numbers of ethylene oxide (EO) units — from O-3 (3 EO, HLB ~6) through O-50 (50 EO, HLB ~18–19). Each additional EO unit adds approximately 0.5 HLB units, enabling precise selection of emulsifier character. Reference benchmark: BASF Lutensol AT series (C16-C18 fatty alcohol ethoxylates).

Low-EO grades (O-3, O-5) function as W/O co-emulsifiers and wetting agents. Mid-EO grades (O-8 to O-10, HLB 12–14) are the primary O/W emulsifiers for skin care creams and lotions — optimal for mineral oils, esters and silicones. High-EO grades (O-20, O-30, O-50) function as solubilisers, incorporating fragrance, essential oils and fat-soluble vitamins into clear aqueous systems without turbidity.

FormEssence EL Series comprises ethoxylated castor oil (EL grades) and ethoxylated hydrogenated castor oil (HEL grades). The triricinolein backbone provides the lipophilic anchor; the polyethylene glycol chains of varying length (10 to 90 EO) determine HLB and solubilisation capacity. HEL-40 (equivalent to BASF Kolliphor RH40 / Cremophor RH40, CAS 61788-85-0) is the pharmaceutical and cosmetic standard for solubilisation of fat-soluble vitamins (Vitamin E, A, D3) and essential oils in clear aqueous formulations — producing transparent solutions at 1–10% loading without clouding. Critical micelle concentration 0.03% w/w @37°C; micelles 10–15 nm diameter. Ph.Eur. / USP-NF monograph compliant.

SorbEssence S-80 is Sorbitan Monooleate — the oleic acid (C18:1, unsaturated) ester of sorbitan, equivalent to Croda Span 80. FormBase SA

Stearic Acid · CAS 57-11-4 · C18 Saturated Fatty Acid

Fatty Acid EmulsifierSelf-EmulsifyingConsistency FactorCOSMOS-eligible

"Stearic Acid — the foundational anionic emulsifier for self-emulsifying cream systems. Provides the fatty acid substrate that forms in-situ soap emulsifiers when neutralised with alkalis (TEA, NaOH, KOH)."

FormBase SA is cosmetic-grade Stearic Acid — the saturated C18 fatty acid, the oldest and most fundamental emulsifier component in personal care. At cosmetic use concentrations (5–15%), stearic acid requires partial or complete in-situ neutralisation with alkali to activate emulsification: when triethanolamine (TEA) is added to a stearic acid-containing O/W emulsion, the fatty acid is partially converted to its stearate soap, which is itself a powerful anionic emulsifier. Typical ratio 1 part TEA to 5 parts stearic acid creates a self-emulsifying system.

The resulting soap-emulsified cream has characteristic properties: slightly alkaline pH (6.5–7.5), waxy-creamy opacity, excellent skin feel and self-emulsifying stability. This system is the basis of traditional cold creams, vanishing creams and basic skin care formulations that remain commercially important in the LATAM mass market, particularly in Brazil, Colombia and Peru where consumer preference for classic cream textures remains strong.

FormBase PEG150

PEG-150 Distearate · CAS 9005-08-7 · Ref: BASF Eumulgin DS

PEG ThickenerSurfactant ThickenerPearl / OpacifierSalt-Free

"PEG-150 Distearate — the standard polymer thickener for anionic surfactant systems, building viscosity in SLES/CAPB shampoos and body washes without electrolyte dependency. Dual function: thickening + pearlescent effect."

FormBase PEG150 is PEG-150 Distearate — the diester of polyethylene glycol (MW ~6600) with stearic acid, equivalent to BASF Eumulgin DS. It is the standard thickener for anionic surfactant cleansing systems, functioning through a micellar interaction mechanism: the PEG chain bridges between surfactant micelles, forming a polymer-micelle network that increases macroscopic viscosity. Unlike salt thickening (NaCl), PEG-150 Distearate thickening is electrolyte-independent and compatible with sensitive formulations where salt would cause instability.

At 1–3% in SLES/CAPB systems, FormBase PEG150 builds consistent viscosity. At higher concentrations, as the wax/fat crystallises during product cooling, it also functions as a pearling agent — forming microscopic crystalline platelets that scatter light within the product, creating the pearlescent appearance of premium shampoos and body washes. This dual thickening/pearling function makes it a formulation-efficient single addition for quality body care and hair care.
