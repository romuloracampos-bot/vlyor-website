The Stability Argument

The formula that
fails quietly.

A cosmetic formula can fail in two ways: visibly — with separation, discolouration or odour that the consumer notices immediately; or quietly — with a preservative system that passes challenge testing at launch but fails in the field six months later, or a Vitamin C concentration that reads 15% on the label but delivers 3% at the point of skin contact.

VLYOR FUNC addresses the chemistry behind both failure modes. ChelaGuard chelating agents prevent the metal-ion catalysis that degrades actives and undermines preservative efficacy. PreserFlex preservative systems provide the microbial challenge protection that keeps products safe throughout their claimed shelf life.

"The formulator's reputation is not defined by the formula they launch. It is defined by the formula that arrives at the consumer twelve months after launch — unchanged, effective, safe."

ChelaGuard 2

Disodium EDTA

Chelating AgentPreservative Booster

"The formula guardian — chelating metal ions that would otherwise destroy your preservative system and degrade your actives."

ChelaGuard 2 is Disodium EDTA at ≥99.0% purity — ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid disodium salt, the most used chelating agent in cosmetics globally. EDTA sequesters divalent metal ions (Ca²⁺, Mg²⁺, Fe²⁺, Cu²⁺) present in tap water, manufacturing equipment and raw materials that would otherwise catalyse oxidative degradation of actives (Vitamin C, retinoids, fragrances) and deactivate preservative systems by binding to their active sites.

The preservative-boosting mechanism is particularly commercially important: EDTA chelates metal ions in bacterial cell walls, destabilising the membrane and rendering bacteria more susceptible to the primary preservative (phenoxyethanol, parabens, DMDM hydantoin). This means ChelaGuard 2 can extend the useful concentration range of preservative systems, reduce the required preservative load and improve broad-spectrum efficacy — enabling formulators to achieve challenge-test compliance at lower preservative doses.

ChelaGuard 4

Tetrasodium EDTA

Chelating AgentHigh-pH Compatible

"ChelaGuard 2's partner for alkaline systems — the chelating agent that remains effective above pH 7."

ChelaGuard 4 is Tetrasodium EDTA at ≥99.0% purity — the fully neutralised tetrasodium salt form of EDTA, differentiated from ChelaGuard 2 (disodium) by its higher pH in solution (pH ~11–12) and improved performance in alkaline and high-pH systems. Where ChelaGuard 2 requires an aqueous system pH above 5 for effective chelation, ChelaGuard 4 maintains chelating activity across a broader pH range including alkaline manufacturing conditions.

Preferred in hair dye systems, bleaching formulations, hair relaxers and alkaline cleansers where system pH is above 7 and disodium EDTA's solubility and chelation efficiency decline. Also used in industrial personal care manufacturing where hard-water control throughout the process — not just in the final product — is required.

SoluCast 40

PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil

SolubiliserO/W EmulsifierClear Systems

"The professional solubiliser for fragrances, essential oils and UV filters in aqueous transparent systems."

SoluCast 40 is PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil — an ethoxylated hydrogenated castor oil derivative widely used as a non-ionic solubiliser and O/W emulsifier in transparent cosmetic systems. Its high HLB value (~13–14) and excellent compatibility with fragrance compounds, essential oils and fat-soluble vitamins make it the standard industrial solubiliser for incorporating oil-soluble ingredients into aqueous formulations while maintaining full transparency.

SoluCast 40 is the preferred solubiliser for UV filters in transparent aqueous systems (toners, mists, transparent serums with SPF), and performs equivalently to polysorbates in most fragrance solubilisation applications. Its castor oil base also provides a mild emollient character that SoluPure polysorbates lack — making it preferred in leave-on transparent formulations where skin conditioning is a secondary objective.

PreserFlex PE

Phenoxyethanol

PreservativeBroad Spectrum

"The global standard cosmetic preservative — broad spectrum, stable and regulatory-accepted worldwide."

PreserFlex PE is Phenoxyethanol at ≥99.5% purity — the single most used cosmetic preservative globally by volume. It provides broad-spectrum antimicrobial activity against bacteria (gram-positive and gram-negative) and yeasts at concentrations of 0.5–1.0%, with a mechanism of action involving disruption of bacterial cell membrane integrity that makes it effective against a wide range of challenge-test organisms.

Phenoxyethanol's dominance in cosmetic preservation stems from its combination of broad-spectrum efficacy, broad formulation compatibility, stability across pH 3–10, and regulatory approval in all major cosmetic markets at up to 1%. It functions as a drop-in preservative in virtually any formulation type and is the baseline ingredient in most combination preservative systems including PreserFlex EHG.

PreserFlex EHG

Phenoxyethanol & Ethylhexylglycerin

Preservative BlendBooster System

"Phenoxyethanol upgraded — the two-component system that closes the gram-positive gap."

PreserFlex EHG is a precision blend of Phenoxyethanol and Ethylhexylglycerin — designed to address the key limitation of pure phenoxyethanol: moderate efficacy against gram-positive bacteria and yeasts at practical use concentrations. Ethylhexylglycerin functions as a membrane-active booster that sensitises microbial cell membranes to phenoxyethanol, enabling full broad-spectrum coverage (bacteria + yeast + mould) at lower total preservative dose than phenoxyethanol alone.

This combination is the most used modern cosmetic preservative system globally — replacing parabens in premium formulations while providing a cleaner, more marketing-friendly label claim than traditional preservatives. Challenge-test (ISO 11930) data typically shows compliant results at combined doses of 0.7–0.9%.

PreserFlex MP

Methylparaben

PreservativeGram-Positive Specialist

"The most studied preservative in history — still the most effective gram-positive anti-fungal available."

PreserFlex MP is Methylparaben at ≥99.5% purity — the most extensively studied cosmetic preservative in the scientific literature, with safety data spanning over 80 years of commercial use. Parabens work via inhibition of microbial enzyme activity, with particular effectiveness against gram-positive bacteria, yeasts and moulds — the organisms most commonly responsible for cosmetic spoilage in water-continuous systems.

Despite consumer and market perception challenges, parabens remain WHO-considered safe at cosmetic use concentrations, and the scientific consensus among dermatologists and toxicologists continues to support their safety. Methylparaben remains the reference standard against which all alternative preservatives are benchmarked for efficacy and skin compatibility. In combination systems, low-dose methylparaben (0.1–0.2%) consistently provides the most robust challenge-test performance.

PreserFlex DMDM

DMDM Hydantoin

PreservativeFormaldehyde Releaser

"Broad-spectrum preservation through controlled formaldehyde release — the industrial standard for rinse-off products."

PreserFlex DMDM is DMDM Hydantoin at ≥99.0% purity — a formaldehyde-releasing preservative that provides broad-spectrum antimicrobial coverage at low use concentrations. It functions by slowly releasing small amounts of formaldehyde in aqueous systems, which binds to and inactivates microbial cell proteins — a mechanism that ensures continuous low-level antimicrobial protection throughout the product shelf life.

PreserFlex DMDM is widely used in rinse-off hair care products, shampoos and conditioners where its excellent performance at pH 5–8 and compatibility with surfactant systems makes it a cost-effective preservative choice. Given market sensitivities around formaldehyde-releasing preservatives in some regions, its use is best positioned in products where the brief prioritises efficacy and cost.

ClimbaFlex

Climbazole

AntifungalAnti-DandruffScalp Treatment

"The imidazole antifungal for scalp care — proven efficacy against Malassezia, the primary cause of dandruff."

ClimbaFlex is Climbazole at ≥99.0% purity — an imidazole antifungal cosmetic active with specific efficacy against Malassezia furfur and related lipophilic yeasts, the primary causative organisms of seborrhoeic dermatitis and dandruff. Climbazole inhibits the ergosterol biosynthesis pathway in fungal cells, disrupting membrane integrity and preventing fungal replication with high selectivity.

ClimbaFlex is the reference cosmetic anti-dandruff active in Europe, used at 0.5% in the majority of branded anti-dandruff shampoos and scalp treatments. Its oil solubility enables efficient deposition on the scalp's sebum-rich environment where Malassezia proliferates, providing sustained antifungal activity between washes that zinc pyrithione and selenium sulfide actives cannot match in residual performance.

AntipersFlex ACH

Aluminum Chlorohydrate

AntiperspirantOTC Active

"The standard antiperspirant active — forming aluminium hydroxide gels that physically reduce eccrine sweat gland secretion."

AntipersFlex ACH is Aluminum Chlorohydrate (ACH) at high purity — the most used antiperspirant active globally, functioning by forming aluminium hydroxide gel plugs within the ductal lumen of eccrine sweat glands, physically restricting sweat secretion to the skin surface. This mechanism is distinctly different from a deodorant (which addresses odour without affecting perspiration volume) — ACH is a true antiperspirant that reduces wetness.

ACH is available in multiple grades (anhydrous and hydrate forms, varying solution concentrations) suited to different formulation formats — roll-on aqueous systems, stick formulations and spray antiperspirants. The 20% solution grade is standard for roll-on products; anhydrous powder grades are used in pressed stick and powder spray formats.

AntipersFlex AZG

Aluminum Zirconium Tetrachlorohydrex Glycine (ZAG)

Enhanced AntiperspirantMaximum Efficacy

"The enhanced-efficacy antiperspirant — ZAG complexes provide superior sweat reduction for clinical and sport-performance products."

AntipersFlex AZG is Aluminum Zirconium Tetrachlorohydrex Glycine (ZAG complex) — the highest-efficacy antiperspirant active commercially available, used in "clinical strength" and "enhanced protection" products where standard ACH does not provide sufficient sweat reduction. The zirconium-aluminium complex forms a more robust, less soluble gel plug in the sweat duct than ACH alone, providing both stronger initial occlusion and longer-lasting antiperspirant performance between applications.

ZAG is the active ingredient in clinical-strength antiperspirant products marketed by leading personal care brands globally. In the growing LATAM performance and clinical personal care segment, it enables product positioning at a significant commercial premium over standard ACH-based formulations.
