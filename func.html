The Stability Argument

The formula that
fails quietly.

A cosmetic formula can fail in two ways. Visibly — with separation, discolouration or odour the consumer notices immediately. Or quietly — with a preservative system that passes challenge testing at launch but succumbs to gram-negative contamination in the field six months later, or an antioxidant whose efficacy was undermined from batch one by iron ions in the manufacturing water supply that no-one measured.

VLYOR FUNC addresses both failure modes at their source. ChelaGuard chelating agents sequester the metal ions that catalyse oxidative degradation and disrupt preservative membranes before they reach the finished formula. PreserFlex systems provide the microbial challenge protection that keeps products safe through their full claimed shelf life — in the ambient temperatures, variable humidity and extended supply chains that characterise LATAM distribution.

"The formulator's commercial reputation is not defined by the formula they launch. It is defined by the formula that arrives at the consumer fourteen months after launch — unchanged, effective, safe."

ChelaGuard 2

Disodium EDTA

Chelating AgentPreservative BoosterAntioxidant Protector

"The invisible guardian of every formula — sequestering the metal ions that catalyse active degradation, disrupt preservation and accelerate rancidity before they cause damage."

ChelaGuard 2 is Disodium EDTA at ≥99.0% purity — the most widely used chelating agent in personal care globally. EDTA forms stable, water-soluble complexes with divalent and trivalent metal cations — calcium, magnesium, iron, copper, manganese — present in trace concentrations in manufacturing water, raw materials and packaging. Left unchelated, these ions catalyse oxidative degradation of actives and systematically undermine preservative function throughout the product shelf life.

The preservative-boosting mechanism is commercially critical: gram-negative bacteria such as Pseudomonas aeruginosa maintain outer membrane structural integrity through divalent cation crosslinks. ChelaGuard 2 chelates these structural cations, destabilising the membrane and dramatically increasing bacterial susceptibility to the primary preservative — often converting a challenge-test failure into a compliant result at the same preservative concentration.

Performance Detail

ChelaGuard 2 is the mandatory addition to any Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid) formulation: iron and copper ions at even single-digit ppm concentrations catalyse ascorbic acid oxidation through Fenton-type reactions. At 0.1–0.3% ChelaGuard 2, this metal-catalysed degradation pathway is comprehensively blocked — extending functional Vitamin C stability from days to months. The same protection applies to retinoids, tocopherols, fragrance components and preservative actives sensitive to metal-catalysed oxidation.

SoluCast 40

PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil

SolubiliserO/W EmulsifierFragrance CompatibleClear Systems

"The professional solubiliser for fragrances, essential oils and oil-soluble UV filters in transparent aqueous systems — with a mild emollient character that polysorbates cannot provide."

SoluCast 40 is PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil (HCO-40) — produced by ethoxylation of hydrogenated castor oil with 40 moles of ethylene oxide. Its high HLB (~13–14) enables effective solubilisation of fragrance compounds, essential oils and fat-soluble vitamins in transparent aqueous systems, maintaining optical clarity at higher oil loads than polysorbate 20 alone.

A practical advantage over polysorbates in leave-on applications is the mild emollient character from the castor oil ester core — the polyhydroxy fatty acid structure contributes a conditioning skin feel that pure polyethoxylate solubilisers lack. This makes SoluCast 40 the preferred solubiliser for transparent toners and clear serums where both optical clarity and positive skin feel are required.

PreserFlex PE

Phenoxyethanol

PreservativeBroad SpectrumParaben-Free

"The global standard cosmetic preservative — broad-spectrum bacteriostatic activity, universal formulation compatibility and regulatory acceptance in every major market at up to 1%."

PreserFlex PE is Phenoxyethanol at ≥99.5% purity — the most commercially used cosmetic preservative globally by formulation frequency. It disrupts microbial cell membranes, altering membrane fluidity and permeability to disable bacterial ATP synthesis. Effective against a broad range of bacteria and yeasts at 0.5–1.0%.

Its dominance in preservation arises from a combination of properties no single alternative fully replicates: broad-spectrum coverage, stability across pH 3–10, compatibility with all formulation matrices and regulatory approval at 1% in every major cosmetic market. It is the backbone of virtually every modern combination preservative system including PreserFlex EHG.

Performance Detail

PE's limitation as a standalone preservative is moderate gram-negative activity at practical concentrations — particularly Pseudomonas species with active efflux pumps. ChelaGuard 2 (EDTA) is therefore the mandatory co-ingredient: EDTA disrupts the Pseudomonas outer membrane, eliminating the structural barrier that enables efflux pump resistance. The PE + EDTA combination reliably passes ISO 11930 gram-negative criteria where PE alone typically fails.

PreserFlex EHG

Phenoxyethanol & Ethylhexylglycerin

Preservative BlendBooster SystemClean LabelISO 11930 Compliant

"Phenoxyethanol upgraded — the modern preservation standard that closes the gram-positive gap with Ethylhexylglycerin membrane sensitisation, delivering full ISO 11930 coverage at lower total preservative dose."

PreserFlex EHG is a precision blend of Phenoxyethanol and Ethylhexylglycerin in a 9:1 ratio — the most commercially dominant modern cosmetic preservation system globally. Ethylhexylglycerin (2-ethylhexyl glycerine ether) functions as a membrane-active sensitiser: its structure inserts into microbial cell membranes, reducing surface tension and disrupting membrane organisation. This sensitisation lowers the MIC of phenoxyethanol for gram-positive organisms — converting resistant strains into susceptible ones.

The combination achieves full ISO 11930 broad-spectrum coverage across all five challenge organisms at combined doses of 0.7–0.9%, versus 0.9–1.0% phenoxyethanol alone. This is the most commercially specified modern preservation system in premium and clean beauty formulations globally, offering paraben-free, formaldehyde-free label claims with proven challenge-test compliance.

Antifungal ActiveAnti-DandruffMalassezia-SpecificOil-Soluble

"The imidazole antifungal for scalp care — clinically effective against Malassezia species, the primary causative organism of dandruff and seborrhoeic dermatitis, with residual activity between washes that water-soluble alternatives cannot match."

ClimbaFlex is Climbazole at ≥99.0% purity — an imidazole antifungal with high specific efficacy against Malassezia furfur, M. globosa and related lipophilic yeast species responsible for seborrhoeic dermatitis and dandruff. Climbazole inhibits the lanosterol 14α-demethylase enzyme in the fungal ergosterol biosynthesis pathway, blocking membrane sterol production with high selectivity for fungal versus mammalian cytochrome P450 enzymes.

ClimbaFlex is the reference cosmetic anti-dandruff active in the European market, used at 0.5% in the majority of premium anti-dandruff shampoos. Its oil solubility enables efficient sebum-mediated deposition on the scalp — the lipid-rich environment where Malassezia preferentially colonises — providing residual antifungal activity between washes. In LATAM markets with high ambient humidity (accelerating Malassezia proliferation), ClimbaFlex-based scalp care represents a significant commercial opportunity.

AntipersFlex ACH

Aluminum Chlorohydrate

Antiperspirant ActiveOTC / CosmeticMultiple Grades

"The standard antiperspirant active globally — forming aluminium hydroxide gel plugs within eccrine sweat gland ducts to physically reduce perspiration at the skin surface."

AntipersFlex ACH is Aluminum Chlorohydrate at ≥20% Al₂O₃ equivalent — the most widely used antiperspirant active globally. ACH functions by forming aluminium hydroxide gel plugs within eccrine gland duct lumens through reaction with secreted sweat, physically restricting sweat secretion to the skin surface. This is a true antiperspirant effect — not odour masking.

Available in multiple grades for different formats: anhydrous powder grades for stick and compressed powder products; 20% and 50% aqueous solution grades for roll-on and gel formats. The solution grades offer simpler manufacturing; anhydrous grades provide higher active loading per weight and are essential for dry-format antiperspirant production.

AntipersFlex AZG

Aluminum Zirconium Tetrachlorohydrex Glycine

Enhanced AntiperspirantClinical StrengthZAG Complex72h Performance

"The highest-efficacy antiperspirant active commercially available — ZAG complex for clinical-strength, sport-performance and extended-wear positioning at a material premium over standard ACH-based systems."

AntipersFlex AZG is Aluminum Zirconium Tetrachlorohydrex Glycine (ZAG complex) — a multi-metal coordination compound where aluminium and zirconium are co-complexed with glycine as a bridging ligand. The ZAG complex forms a denser, less hydrophilic and mechanically more robust gel plug within the eccrine sweat duct versus ACH alone, providing stronger initial occlusion and significantly longer-lasting efficacy between applications.

ZAG is the active ingredient in every major clinical-strength, 72-hour and sport antiperspirant marketed globally. In the growing LATAM premium antiperspirant segment — driven by high ambient temperature and sportswear lifestyle positioning — ZAG-based products command significant price premium and expanding market share. AntipersFlex AZG is supplied in anhydrous powder form for dry-manufacturing stick, aerosol and compressed formats.
