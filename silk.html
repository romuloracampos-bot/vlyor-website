The Silicone Argument

The ingredient that
performs at the
molecular level.

No organic oil or ester delivers what silicone delivers. The siloxane backbone — alternating Si-O bonds with methyl side chains — gives silicones a combination of properties that organic chemistry cannot replicate in a single molecule: a friction coefficient lower than any natural oil, a surface tension of ~20 mN/m (versus 30+ for organic esters), heat stability to 200°C+, and near-zero biological reactivity. These are not incidental properties — they are the structural consequences of the Si-O bond's unique geometry and bond energy, which organic C-C and C-O bonds cannot match.

In formulation practice, silicone selection is sensory engineering. A moisturiser with SilkFluid 100 cSt spreads instantly and dries to a clean, breathable skin feel. The same formula with SilkFluid 12500 cSt becomes rich, structured and slow-spreading — a completely different sensory identity from a single ingredient change. Add SilkLume and every surface the formula contacts reflects more light. Replace part of the oil phase with SilkVeil and the formula dries in seconds to a powder-dry finish that no organic ingredient can produce.

"Silicone selection is sensory architecture. You are not choosing an emollient — you are deciding how fast the product spreads, how it feels at noon, whether it shines, and whether it disappears. These are creative decisions with molecular instruments."

"The lightest dimethicone — 100 cSt polydimethylsiloxane delivering maximum slip with the driest, most water-like sensory profile of the PDMS fluid range."

SilkFluid 100 is Dimethicone at 100 cSt viscosity — a 100% active, linear polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) fluid produced to cosmetic-grade purity standards meeting established PDMS 100 cSt specifications. At this viscosity, the silicone chain length produces a fluid that behaves more like water than oil in handling — spreading instantly across skin, delivering immediate slip and lubricity, then settling to a clean, breathable, non-greasy skin feel within seconds. The surface tension of ~20 mN/m — well below any organic oil — drives this extraordinary initial spread.

SilkFluid 100 is the primary dimethicone grade for antiperspirant/deodorant aerosols, spray sunscreens, facial primers and lightweight day moisturisers where a fast-spreading, non-heavy application experience is non-negotiable. It is also the standard carrier for dissolving and uniformly dispersing UV filters, pigments and active ingredients in the oil phase before emulsification, where its low viscosity ensures homogeneous distribution before the manufacturing process.

The 100 cSt grade provides the highest spreadability and lowest tack of the dimethicone fluid range. SilkVeil

Cyclopentasiloxane (D5)

Volatile SiliconeCarrier / DeliveryDry-DownEU Restriction 2027

"The volatile silicone carrier — Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) disperses actives across skin uniformly then evaporates completely, leaving only the deposited benefit and a powder-dry sensory finish impossible to replicate with organic ingredients."

SilkVeil is Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) — the benchmark volatile silicone for cosmetic application. Its cyclic siloxane structure creates a compound with a boiling point of ~210°C but significant evaporation at skin temperature (37°C) — the evaporation rate at body temperature produces the characteristic dry, instantly non-greasy finish of modern skincare, sunscreen and body care products. SilkVeil evaporates from the skin surface within seconds to minutes of application, leaving no residual film beyond the materials it has deposited.

As a precision delivery carrier, SilkVeil dissolves and disperses non-volatile actives, pigments, UV filters and emollients uniformly across skin during the evaporation phase, then disappears to leave only the deposited material. This mechanism produces fundamentally better active distribution and a dramatically lighter skin feel than equivalent formulations using non-volatile silicone fluids or organic solvents as carriers. It is the ingredient behind the dry-touch, transfer-resistant finish of modern foundations, the light application of spray sunscreens and the non-greasy feel of premium body care.

SilkLume

Phenyl Trimethicone

Phenyl SiliconeOptical ShineSPF BoosterColour Enhancer

"The optical performance silicone — Phenyl Trimethicone's high refractive index delivers measurable hair shine enhancement, SPF boosting and colour cosmetic gloss that polydimethylsiloxane fluids cannot provide."

SilkLume is Phenyl Trimethicone — a polyphenylmethylsiloxane a polyphenylmethylsiloxane produced to cosmetic-grade specifications with refractive index ~1.46. The substitution of phenyl groups onto the siloxane backbone fundamentally changes the optical properties of the molecule: phenyl groups have a significantly higher refractive index (~1.50) than the methyl groups of PDMS (~1.40), producing a finished fluid with a refractive index of ~1.46 — substantially higher than dimethicone. This optical differentiation is the basis of SilkLume's commercial value: when applied to hair or skin, it reflects more light, creating a measurably brighter, glossier appearance that consumers perceive as healthy, youthful and luminous.

Beyond optical performance, SilkLume provides SPF enhancement in sun care formulations — its higher refractive index improves UV scatter from inorganic filters (titanium dioxide, zinc oxide), increasing SPF values by 10–20% at equivalent filter concentrations. It also improves pigment dispersion and colour intensity in colour cosmetics through its superior organic ingredient compatibility and wetting properties compared to PDMS. Additionally, its excellent solubility in ethanol (up to 50% at 4°C) makes it uniquely suitable for alcohol-based hair spray formulations.

SilkLume's phenyl groups also provide superior oxidation resistance versus PDMS — the phenyl ring system is more resistant to thermal and UV-induced oxidative degradation than methyl groups. Additionally, the non-oily, dry-touch emolliency characteristic of Phenyl Trimethicone — despite its high refractive index — enables optical shine without the heavy, greasy feel that high-loading PDMS systems produce, making it ideal for fine hair applications and lightweight leave-on serums where performance without weight is the brief.

SilkAqua

PEG-12 Dimethicone

Water-Soluble SiliconeAqueous SystemsSilicone EmulsifierClear Serums

"The silicone that dissolves in water — PEG-12 Dimethicone brings silicone slip, conditioning and emolliency to clear aqueous serums, gels and water-based formulations where conventional silicones cannot go."

SilkAqua is PEG-12 Dimethicone — a polyether-modified silicone produced by the copolymerisation of PDMS segments with polyethylene glycol chains. This hybrid architecture gives SilkAqua a unique property: it is simultaneously silicone-functional and freely water-miscible. Standard dimethicone is completely incompatible with aqueous phases — it separates immediately and requires emulsification. SilkAqua disperses homogeneously into water, enabling the silicone's slip, conditioning and breathable film properties to be delivered directly from aqueous formulations — clear gels, water serums, toners and lotion systems — without emulsification or any special processing.

SilkAqua also functions as a silicone emulsifier and wetting agent — its amphiphilic PEG-dimethicone structure lowers the interfacial tension between silicone and water phases, acting as a compatibiliser that stabilises silicone-in-water emulsions. This enables formulators to incorporate higher levels of non-water-soluble silicones into aqueous formulations while maintaining clarity and stability.

SilkMatrix

Dimethicone Crosspolymer (Silicone Elastomer Gel)

Elastomer GelVelvet Skin FeelPore-Blur EffectRheology Modifier

"The velvet architecture — Dimethicone Crosspolymer elastomer gel delivers the unique powder-dry, instantly-smooth, pore-blurring skin feel that defines modern primers, luxury foundations and prestige skin care serums."

SilkMatrix is a Dimethicone Crosspolymer silicone elastomer gel — crosslinked dimethicone swollen in a silicone carrier fluid, producing a three-dimensional network of silicone polymer that behaves as a soft, deformable solid. This unique physical structure — neither a fluid nor a solid but an elastic gel — delivers a distinctly different and commercially unmatched sensory profile: it absorbs quickly onto skin (faster than any silicone fluid), dries to a soft, matte, powder-like finish that physically fills and blurs the appearance of pores and fine lines, and imparts a velvety slip entirely unlike the lubricious feel of fluid silicones.

As a rheology modifier, SilkMatrix fundamentally changes formula texture with small additions. At 2–5%, it transforms watery serums into smooth, glossy gels; it converts creamy emulsions into structured, bouncy textures; and it provides oil-absorbing properties that reduce formula shine without the matte chalkiness of powder-based oil absorbers. This multi-function — sensory modifier, texture builder, pore-filler — makes SilkMatrix one of the most commercially versatile single ingredients in premium cosmetic formulation.

SilkDuo 1501

Cyclopentasiloxane (and) Dimethiconol

Gum Blend (D5)Film-FormerSplit-End RepairEU D5 Restriction 2027

"Ultra-high molecular weight dimethiconol in volatile D5 carrier — the gum blend for film-forming, split-end repair and durable multi-wash conditioning in hair care and protective skin treatment."

SilkDuo 1501 is approximately 15% of an ultra-high molecular weight dimethiconol silicone gum dissolved in Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) — a pourable, processable fluid that delivers ultra-HMW silicone gum performance within standard cosmetic manufacturing processes. Dimethiconol is the hydroxyl-terminated form of polydimethylsiloxane (HO-PDMS-OH); at ultra-high molecular weight, it behaves as a silicone gum — a viscoelastic solid with extraordinary conditioning substantivity. Dissolved in D5 at 15%, this gum becomes a pourable, processable fluid that delivers the full conditioning performance of ultra-HMW silicone gum in a format compatible with standard cosmetic manufacturing.

SilkDuo 1501's formulation value is its two-stage delivery mechanism: the D5 carrier evaporates rapidly during application, uniformly distributing the dissolved dimethiconol gum across the skin or hair surface; as D5 departs, it deposits a highly substantive, coherent dimethiconol film — more durable than any fluid silicone grade — that persists through washing, humidity and styling. In hair care, this mechanism creates exceptional split-end repair, shine, anti-frizz and heat protection from a single raw material. The dimethiconol gum's hydroxyl end-groups provide greater surface affinity than methyl-terminated PDMS, contributing to better wash-off resistance.

SilkDuo 1401

Cyclotetrasiloxane (and) Cyclopentasiloxane (and) Dimethiconol

Gum Blend (D4+D5)Bi-Phase EvaporationWater-Resistant FilmEU D4+D5 Restriction 2027

"Dimethiconol gum in a D4/D5 cyclomethicone carrier blend — two-stage evaporation kinetics for sophisticated dry-down character, superior substantivity and water-resistant film formation."

SilkDuo 1401 is approximately 13% of an ultra-high molecular weight dimethiconol in a blend of Cyclotetrasiloxane (D4) and Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) — a pourable cosmetic gum blend providing dual-carrier volatile delivery with superior substantivity. The D4/D5 carrier blend was specifically selected to prevent freezing (D4 alone has a higher melting point that can cause solidification) while maintaining the mobile, pourable consistency necessary for cosmetic processing. This dual-cyclomethicone carrier creates a two-stage evaporation profile that distinguishes SilkDuo 1401 from the single-carrier SilkDuo 1501.

The D4 component has faster evaporation kinetics than D5 — it begins departing the film immediately upon application, providing rapid initial dry-down. D5 evaporates more slowly, extending the application window and providing the second phase of active deposition before the gum film is fully deposited. This sequential evaporation creates a more sophisticated, progressive sensory transition — immediate lightness (D4 evaporation), then extended conditioning feel (D5 evaporation), then the final durable gum deposit — that distinguishes premium hair treatments and prestige skin formulations. SilkDuo 1401 also provides measurably superior water-resistant film formation compared to SilkDuo 1501, enabling the formulation of water-resistant sunscreens and long-wear cosmetics with silicone gum technology.

SilkGel 9045

Cyclopentasiloxane (and) Dimethicone Crosspolymer

Elastomer in D5Oil-Phase ThickenerDry SmoothnessEU D5 Restriction 2027

"Dimethicone crosspolymer elastomer in volatile D5 carrier — the silicone-phase thickener for anhydrous sunscreens and volatile silicone systems. Cold-processable, shear-thinning, no balling effect on skin."

SilkGel 9045 is approximately 12.5% Dimethicone Crosspolymer silicone elastomer in Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) — a shear-thinning, cold-processable elastomer gel for silicone and oil-phase thickening. Where SilkMatrix (the existing VLYOR elastomer gel) uses a non-volatile dimethicone carrier, SilkGel 9045 uses volatile D5 as carrier — a fundamental difference that changes the formulation behaviour: the D5 carrier makes SilkGel 9045 a shear-thinning, pourable gel that can be added cold to formulations, disperses easily in silicone-phase systems, and evaporates from the finished product on skin application — leaving only the crosspolymer elastomer particles as the functional deposit.

As an oil-phase and silicone-phase thickener, SilkGel 9045 is technically irreplaceable: it thickens cyclosiloxane and silicone fluid phases directly without requiring heating or additional emulsifiers. This makes it the standard thickener for anhydrous silicone sunscreen formulations — where thick, pourable silicone gels must maintain stability without separation — and for water-in-silicone emulsions where the silicone continuous phase must be structured to prevent creaming. The crosspolymer particles' shear-thinning behaviour (flows under shear, recovers on rest) also contributes to excellent spreadability on skin with immediate recovery to the stable gel structure.
