The Silicone Argument

Premium feel is
a material property,
not a claim.

The luxury cosmetics consumer interacts with a product first through touch and second through vision. Before any active ingredient has time to work, the consumer has already formed a quality judgment based on how the product spreads, how quickly it absorbs, what feel it leaves and what optical effect it produces. This judgment is determined almost entirely by silicone selection.

No other ingredient category provides the range of sensory outcomes available through silicone technology: the ultra-dry evaporative finish of volatile cyclopentasiloxane; the extraordinary slip of standard dimethicone; the powdery-velvety signature of elastomer gels; the intense specular shine of high-refractive phenyl trimethicone. VLYOR SILK provides all of these — and the technical guidance to combine them intelligently.

"The consumer cannot identify the silicone in the formula. But they describe the product as 'luxurious', 'silky', 'professional'. SILK is the ingredient that earns those words."

SilkFluid Series

Dimethicone

Emollient Silicone4 Viscosity GradesFoundation of Sensory Design

"The most used cosmetic silicone — viscosity-selectable from lightweight serum fluidity to rich cream density."

SilkFluid Series provides Dimethicone (polydimethylsiloxane, PDMS) in four viscosity grades: 100, 350, 1000 and 12500 cSt — covering the full range from ultra-light fluid to viscous silicone that anchors heavy textures. Dimethicone is the most used silicone in cosmetics, present in virtually every formulation category from primers and foundations to moisturisers, hair serums and sunscreens.

Its cosmetic value is defined by three properties: extraordinary skin slip (silicone's friction coefficient against skin is lower than any organic oil), breathable moisture retention (it forms a semi-occlusive film that reduces TEWL without the complete occlusion of petrolatum), and exceptional compatibility with virtually all cosmetic ingredients. The viscosity grade selection determines the sensory profile: 100 cSt for dry-touch fluid application; 350 cSt for balanced all-purpose emolliency; 1000 cSt for rich, silky textures; 12500 cSt for structured, spreadable systems.

SilkVeil

Cyclopentasiloxane

Volatile SiliconeDry-Touch CarrierD5

"The carrier that disappears — delivering actives to skin with a dry, powdery finish and no residual feel."

SilkVeil is Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) — the benchmark volatile silicone for cosmetic application. Its exceptional volatility at skin temperature (boiling point ~210°C, significant evaporation rate at 37°C) means that SilkVeil evaporates from the skin surface within seconds to minutes of application, leaving no residual film — a property that enables the "dry-touch" sensory experience that defines modern moisturisers, foundations, sunscreens and body care products.

SilkVeil's primary function is as a high-performance cosmetic carrier: it dissolves and uniformly disperses non-volatile actives, pigments, UV filters and emollients across the skin surface during the evaporation phase, then disappears to leave only the deposited material. This delivery mechanism produces significantly better active distribution and a dramatically lighter skin feel than equivalent formulations based on organic solvents or non-volatile silicone fluids.

SilkDuo 1501

Cyclopentasiloxane & Dimethiconol

Silicone BlendVolatile + Fixed

"Volatile carrier + substantive conditioning film — the pre-blended combination for conditioner and hair serum development."

SilkDuo 1501 is a pre-optimised blend of Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) and Dimethiconol — combining the evaporative carrier function of D5 with the substantive, non-volatile conditioning film of high-MW dimethiconol in a single formulation-ready raw material. The D5 component ensures smooth, even initial distribution across hair or skin; as it evaporates, the dimethiconol remains behind as a durable conditioning film that provides slip, frizz control, moisture retention and protection from mechanical damage.

SilkDuo 1501 is widely used in rinse-out conditioners, leave-in treatments and styling products where a non-greasy conditioning deposit is the desired outcome. Its pre-blended nature simplifies formulation and ensures consistent volatile/fixed silicone ratios across production batches.

SilkDuo 1401

Cyclotetrasiloxane, Cyclopentasiloxane & Dimethiconol

Complex Silicone BlendD4/D5 + Dimethiconol

"Three-component silicone blend with enhanced spread and more nuanced evaporation profile."

SilkDuo 1401 combines Cyclotetrasiloxane (D4), Cyclopentasiloxane (D5) and Dimethiconol in a pre-blended system that provides a more complex evaporation kinetics compared to SilkDuo 1501. The addition of D4 (lower boiling point than D5) creates a two-stage evaporation profile: rapid initial D4 evaporation provides immediate dry-down and active deposition, followed by slower D5 evaporation for sustained conditioning feel, leaving the dimethiconol conditioning film as the final skin/hair deposit.

Preferred in premium hair treatment formulations where a more sophisticated, progressive sensory transition is desired, and in facial primers where a two-stage dry-down creates a distinctive application experience.

SilkDuo 503

Dimethicone & Dimethiconol

Fixed Silicone BlendNon-VolatileLong-lasting

"Non-volatile silicone combination for lasting conditioning — the fixed silicone system for products with no dry-touch requirement."

SilkDuo 503 is a blend of Dimethicone and Dimethiconol — both non-volatile silicone components — providing a durable, persistent emollient conditioning film without the evaporation step. This non-volatile character makes it the preferred silicone for heavy-duty emollient systems, barrier creams, occlusive body butters and extremely dry skin care, where the sustained presence of the silicone film is the therapeutic mechanism rather than a sensory limitation.

The dimethiconol component provides cross-linking potential on the skin/hair surface, creating a more substantive, coherent film compared to dimethicone alone — improving water-resistance and durability of the emollient deposit.

SilkAqua

PEG-12 Dimethicone

Hydrophilic SiliconeWater-CompatibleO/W Systems

"The water-compatible silicone — bridging the phase barrier for silicone inclusion in aqueous and emulsion systems."

SilkAqua is PEG-12 Dimethicone — a polyether-modified silicone with the unique property of being both water-soluble and silicone-functional. Standard dimethicone is incompatible with aqueous phases, limiting its direct use in water-based serums and gels. SilkAqua's PEG modification makes it freely water-miscible, enabling the inclusion of silicone's slip, conditioning and emollient properties in clear aqueous formulations, water-based serums and gel textures where conventional silicones cannot be incorporated.

SilkAqua also functions as an emulsifier and wetting agent in silicone-in-water systems, stabilising silicone emulsions and improving the spreadability and wettability of formulations containing non-polar components.

SilkLume

Phenyl Trimethicone

High-Refractive SiliconeOptical ShineLuxury Hair & Skin

"The optical silicone — light refraction engineered into a liquid for maximum shine in every application."

SilkLume is Phenyl Trimethicone — a phenyl-modified silicone with a refractive index of ~1.460–1.465, significantly higher than standard dimethicone (1.400). This elevated refractive index means SilkLume interacts differently with visible light: it produces a stronger, more intense specular reflection when applied as a film on hair or skin, delivering a "wet look" shine and optical vibrancy that conventional silicone fluids cannot achieve.

In hair care, SilkLume is the primary active in salon-quality shine serums, gloss sprays and glossy leave-in treatments — the ingredient behind the "salon-freshly-blown-out" optical appearance that consumers associate with premium hair care. In skin care, it provides a subtle luminous glow effect in facial moisturisers and body oils where optical enhancement is a desired aesthetic benefit without the white-cast of mica-based luminisers.

SilkMatrix

Cyclopentasiloxane & Dimethicone Crosspolymer

Silicone ElastomerSensory GelTexture Modifier

"The silicone elastomer that transforms textures — creating the distinctive powdery-velvety feel of premium skin care."

SilkMatrix is a silicone elastomer gel comprising Dimethicone Crosspolymer dispersed in Cyclopentasiloxane — the ingredient responsible for the distinctive powdery, velvety, silky skin feel that defines premium skin care textures. The crosslinked dimethicone polymer forms a three-dimensional elastomer network that, when incorporated into formulations, creates a distinctive "second-skin" sensory effect — a smooth, velvety, powdery-dry finish that no other cosmetic ingredient replicates.

SilkMatrix is used in premium facial moisturisers, primers, sunscreens and body lotions to deliver the sensory signature that consumers associate with luxury cosmetics. It also functions as a physical mattifier — the elastomer particles scatter light and absorb excess sebum, providing an immediate soft-focus, pore-minimising visual effect in addition to its unique tactile properties.
